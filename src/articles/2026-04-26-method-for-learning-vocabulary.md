    <h2 id="workflow">Workflow</h2>

    ### 1. Select 60 new words
    Resource: Excel

    ### 2. Classify these words
    *ChatGPT prompt*
    Please classify the following words thematically. Separate the function words from the rest, and then organize the remaining words into three distinct stories (let me know when more stories would be needed), ensuring that each story incorporates its assigned words naturally into a meaningful context. The words are:
    - ...

    Please fill the 2nd column of the 2-columns table by adding the story topic next to each word, in the following order. For functions word, just add "0. Function word":
    - ...

    ### 3. Create stories using these words
    *ChatGPT prompt*
    My goal is to learn Chinese vocabulary through stories. Please create a story using the following list of new words (HSK3 level), making sure they repeat throughout the text, and highlight them in bold. Make it long enough so the new words concentration is around 5-15%, and so they are repeated enough times for long term memory. The other words in the story should be HSK3 or below. The story should also make sense and be coherent. Please give the story a title (also in Chinese).

    Try to include as many of these:
    Word 1
    Word 2
    Word 3

    Must include all these:
    Word 1
    Word 2
    Word 3

    ### 4. Review
    Format in word and review the stories. 
    (see *Progress Tracking* for more info.)

    Steps to track vocabulary learned:
    1. Create the story (refer to *Workflow*) 								— Sunday 0

    4. Check the words again without meaning to see if i really learned them 	— Sunday 3

    Note: number indicates relative week number




    <h2 id="applied">Applied</h2>

    Objective: Create a coherent, engaging Chinese story (HSK3 level) that maximizes repetition of provided vocabulary words to aid memorization, while keeping other words simple (HSK3 or below).
    Requirements:
    1. Length & Repetition:
      1. 600-1000+ characters (long enough for natural repetition).
      2. Each target word appears 5-10+ times.
      3. Highlight all target words in bold for visibility.
    2. Vocabulary Integration: Use words in multiple contexts (dialogue, narration, descriptions).
    3. Natural Flow:
      1. 5-15% target word density (avoid forced repetition).
      2. Weave words into plot events.
    4. Title: Include in Chinese
    5. Mandatory Words (must include all):
    白（副）	bái	without results; in vain; waste efforts
    不得不	bùdébù	Cannot but
    曾经	céngjīng	once
    朝	cháo	towards
    将来	jiānglái	future
    较	jiào	compare; comparatively; relatively; fairly; quite; rather
    恐怕	kǒngpà	I’m afraid
    缺	quē	be short of; lack
    通常	tōngcháng	usually
    由	yóu	from
